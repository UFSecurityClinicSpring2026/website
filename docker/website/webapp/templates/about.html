About Us

Who We Are

The Florida Cybersecurity Clinic (FCSC) is a digital security clinic comprised of students and faculty at the University of Florida. Through a model similar to university clinics in law and medicine, we train teams of students to help Florida non-profits, public agencies, and local organizations reduce their vulnerability to cyberattacks. Our goal is to protect our communities from cyber threats and build the next generation of cybersecurity leaders.

What We Do

We work directly with clients to assess their cyber incident preparedness and suggest low-cost improvements appropriate to their context. Our approach is based on Defensive Social Engineering and considers technical and non-technical facets to cybersecurity. FCSC performs these services at no cost to our clients.
